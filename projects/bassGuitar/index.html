Bass Guitar Build Log

Since I still have a lot of black walnut and padauk left over from my previous guitar build, I decided to build a bass guitar as side project during my 4th semester.

On the right, you can see some basic specifications of the bass that I've decided on at the beginning of the project. These specs were chosen at the beginning to give me a general idea of the design and layout of the build.

Specification Value
Scale Length 34"
Pickup Layout PJ
Neck Style Neck Through
Neck Wood Padauk
Body Wood Black Walnut and Padauk

You'll notice that at this stage, the only dimension I've picked out is the scale length of the build. That is because I don't want to restrict the design process too much yet, so only choosing a scale length early allows me to explore design options.
